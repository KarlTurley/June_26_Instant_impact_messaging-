Rules of language use
Brand layer

Talent Function · Focus · Flex

  • Website, manifesto, About page
  • Brand films, keynotes, events
  • Top-of-funnel content and social
  • Email signatures, deck covers
  • PR and analyst briefings
Commercial layer

Embedded RPO · Partial RPO · Lead PSL

  • Sales conversations from minute one
  • Pitch deck content and proposals
  • Contracts, MSAs, PSL agreements
  • Procurement and RFP responses
  • Outbound emails and partner talks
The single hardest rule
Sales opens with the commercial term. Always. The biggest risk in dual-track is sales reverting to brand language to sound proprietary. "Our Embedded RPO offer — we call it Talent Function — could replace your current PSL spend in year one" lands.
